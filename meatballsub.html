Meatball Sandwich


Description

This is an original, homemade recipe created after much trial and error.

Simple but delicious, you won't be disappointed by the outcome!

Please give it a go yourself and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Deli Buns
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic Butter
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Set burner to "4" and place pot on stove.
  2. Fill pot with marinara sauce and let sit for 15 minutes, stirring every few minutes.
  3. Put frozen meatballs (or homemade if you prefer) into pot and continue to occassionally stir.
  4. Preheat oven to 375F
  5. Cover a baking sheet in aluminum foil.
  6. Place deli buns onto baking sheet and spread garlic butter and onto inside of buns
  7. Put 1-2 slices of Provolone cheese inside of deli buns depending on size of buns.
  8. Once oven is done preheating, place baking sheet inside oven and bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese is fully melted.
  9. Remove baking sheet and check pot to make sure meatballs are fully cooked.
  10. Pour meatballs and marinara sauce onto inside of deli buns.
  11. Sprinkle parmesan cheese and Parsley on top for finishing touch.
  12. Let cool for a few minutes and enjoy!
