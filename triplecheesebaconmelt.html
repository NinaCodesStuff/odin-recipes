Triple Cheese Bacon Melt

Description

Delcious and easy to make, this recipe is great for anyone looking for a quick treat.

Enjoy the cheesey goodness!

Ingredients

  • 6 Slices of Bread
  • Bacon Strips
  • 3 Slices of Provolone cheese
  • 3 Slices of Cheddar Cheese
  • 3 Slices of Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take cheeses out of fridge and let sit for 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 400F and cover baking sheet in aluminum foil.
  3. Arrange bacon strips on baking sheet and cook in oven for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Once deeply golden, remove from oven and allow to cool and crisp.
  5. Spread butter on each side of bread slices.
  6. Place 3 slices of bread into skillet and layer 1 of each cheese onto each slice.
  7. Put 2 slices of bacon on top.
  8. Turn burner to "4" and grill the sandwiches, making sure each side is cooked.
  9. Remove from skillet and let cool for a few minutes.
  10. Cut sandwiches in half and serve!
