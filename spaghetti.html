Spaghetti

Description

Quick, simple, and classic recipe!

Perfect dinner after a long day at work.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Take out ground beef and cook in pan on "5" setting until no longer pink.
  2. Put pot on burner and set to "4"
  3. Pour marinara sauce, 2 tbsp of salt and 1 tbsp of pepper into pot and stir. Let sit 15 minutes.
  4. While waiting, fill dutch oven 3/4 full with water and set burner to "7".
  5. After 15 minutes place ground beef and frozen meatballs into pot with marinara sauce.
  6. Let continue to cook for 10 more minutes.
  7. Once water in dutch over is boiling, put spaghetti noodles inside and stir occassionally.
  8. After 5-10 minutes, or when noodles are floppy, pour noodles into spaghetti strainer and let cool.
  9. Once cooled and once meatballs are fully cooked, combine marinara sauce, meatballs, spaghetti and ground beef into bowl
  10. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
  11. Enjoy!
